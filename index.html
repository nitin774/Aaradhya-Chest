FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the common symptoms of asthma? ▾

Common asthma symptoms include shortness of breath, chest tightness, wheezing, and persistent coughing—especially at night or early morning. If you experience these, consult Dr. Ankita Verma for proper diagnosis and treatment.

Do I need a prior appointment to visit the clinic? ▾

While walk-ins are accepted, we recommend booking an appointment to avoid waiting. You can call +91 8433 123456 or book via WhatsApp for same-day appointments.

What is a Pulmonary Function Test (PFT)? ▾

A PFT measures how well your lungs work. It includes spirometry, lung volume, and diffusion tests. Dr. Verma uses these results to diagnose and monitor conditions like asthma, COPD, and restrictive lung disease.

Is the clinic equipped for COVID-19 and post-COVID lung issues? ▾

Yes, we provide comprehensive post-COVID respiratory care including lung function assessment, rehabilitation, and treatment for lingering symptoms like breathlessness and fatigue.

What should I bring for my first consultation? ▾

Please bring any previous medical reports, chest X-rays, CT scans, current medications list, and your ID proof. This helps us provide accurate and faster diagnosis.
